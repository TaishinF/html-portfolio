The Best Movies According to Taishin

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

1. Shoplifters

This is my favorite Japanese film by Hirokazu Kore-eda. The theme of this movie is modern Japanese society and family. This movie makes us seriously think about the hidden dark side of modern Japan.

2. My Tomorrow, Your Yesterday

This is a Japanese romantic drama film I really recommend to you.

3. Yes Man

This must be the best romantic comedy film from the US. I really recommend it.
